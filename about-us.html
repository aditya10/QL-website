OUR MISSON

QLeap’s mission is to educate and empower individuals and organizations with 21st century skills and competencies to achieve a quantum growth.

To achieve this mission, QLeap focuses on Individual and Organizational Growth by developing skills, behaviours & expertize in

  • Emotion - Mindset and Behaviour
  • Innovation - Skills and Competencies
  • Technology - Knowledge and Expertise

EDUCATION & CONSULTING

We are here to help organisations and individuals to achieve a Quantum Leap in business and professional growth.

QLeap offers two key services.

  • Education
    On-the-campus and online executive courses in partnership with global universities for family business owners, professionals, startup founders & students to help them acquire knowledge and skills on creativity, innovation, technology and entrepreneurship
  • Consulting
    Developing and execution of outcome driven innovation strategy, roadmap, programs for Indian and global corporates to achieve disruptive innovations for developed and emerging markets. Designing and developing innovation & entrepreneurship programs for colleges/universities
