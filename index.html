Creating 21st Century Professionals & Leaders

QLeap Academy of Innovation & Entrepreneurship

The world is changing rapidly in every sphere of life, from technology to business to economics, at a never imagined speed. Advances in artificial intelligence, robotics, additive manufacturing, biotech & automation are disrupting almost every industry. Businesses are finding it increasingly difficult to survive and grow during this rapid transformation. This tectonic shift also has a profound impact on the jobs of tomorrow - majority of the students today will end up doing jobs that don't exist yet. The world is transforming its conventional thinking on the way we live, work, and on what it takes to succeed.

These rapid changes are compelling businesses, professionals and students in India and around the world to acquire new age skills and competencies to be relevant and successful in times of uncertainty.

QLeap’s mission is to educate and empower individuals and organizations with 21st century skills and competencies to achieve quantum growth through our innovative education and consulting services. We seek to create a new generation of students, professionals and entrepreneurs equipped with new-age skills & competencies to enhance creative, innovative and entrepreneurial thinking power.
