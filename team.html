Dr. Aravind Chinchure

  • Founder and CEO

Dr. Aravind Chinchure is a renowned academician, a corporate strategist and an innovation leader. He is an expert in the formulation of corporate and governmental policies that influence innovation and research. He holds a PhD in Physics, and has an experience spanning 25 years in R&D, innovation, intellectual property, startup venture investment, policy, social development and teaching.

Dr. Chinchure has worked with many global innovation thought leaders and Nobel laureates on projects of national and regional significance. He credits these associations for shaping his perceptions and expertise around results-driven innovations. He was part of Indian and global research organizations, and large multinational and Indian companies in diverse technology areas, driving innovation in technology as well as leading intellectual property-driven assets creation at workplaces.

He is credited with designing and implementing India’s first two-year residential and experiential MBA course on Innovation and Entrepreneurship, at Symbiosis Institute of Business Management (SIBM), Pune. Currently, Dr. Chinchure is the Chair Professor of Innovation and Entrepreneurship at the Symbiosis International University, Pune, India.

Dr. Rajiv Kumar

Rajiv Kumar was a Chief Scientist and Head of Tata Chemicals Innovation Centre (TCIC). Till recently, he served as a Consultant to Tata Chemicals. Before joining Tata Chemicals, he worked at National Chemical Laboratory (NCL), Pune for more than 26 years. He has led several new technology development programs that resulted into are first-to-India/world innovative products and solutions.

Currently, he teaches innovation and technology management to the students of Symbiosis Institute of Business Management, Pune. He has received ChemCon Distinguished Scientist award 2008, Dr. K.G. Naik Gold medal award for excellence in Chemical Sciences and Technology for 2003, Best Scientist of the year Award of NCL Research Foundation in 1997 and Young Scientist Award, Catalysis Society of India in 1994.

He has around 200 papers published in scientific journals and more than 80 patents to his credit.

Rajiv Prakash

Rajiv Prakash has over fifteen years of internet business and eCommerce experience at Future Group, Cisco, Andersen and KPMG. He formerly led Future Group’s eCommerce businesses including Futurebazaar’s turnaround. Prior to Futurebazaar, he co-founded a retail technology and services venture, Future Knowledge Services, in association with Future Group.

Since 2012, he helps innovative startups and their founders scale, and advises retailers and consumer brands on staying relevant to the omni-channel consumer. Relationships include Martjack, Reverie Language Technologies, TeaBox, Greenlight Planet, Goli Vada Pav, BoonBox, iPay, Find Me A Shoe, Nykaa and Pidilite. He also advises investors on internet sector investments.

He also works with development sector startups through the social sector incubator, UnLtd. Rajiv holds a post-graduate diploma in management from the Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad.

Dr. Shyam Vasudevrao

Shyam Vasudevrao holds a doctorate in Real Time Embedded Systems, specialising in Parallel Computer Architecture from Indian Institute of Science (gold Medal) & has been with CG Smith, Ericsson, Tata Consultancy Services and as Director of Technology at Philips.

He was instrumental in devising one of the first hardware based antivirus solution in 90s.

In his illustrious career Dr. Shyam has established university relationships for joint research programs to work on wireless technology and medical electronics with IISc, IIT Mumbai, IIT Karagpur, IIT Gowhathi, Maastricht University & Alborg University.

While at Philips he has established the innovation framework for Consumer Electronics and Medical Systems division. He is a serial innovator and entrepreneur. His innovation 3nethra, an all in one eye screening device, at Forus has won several awards.

He has filed over 20 international patents for his inventions and closely associated with startup ecosystems in India and Europe.

R. Sridhar

Sridhar is an Innovation Facilitator, Consultant & Creativity Coach, who helps his clients benefit from the power of ideas. He helps clients choose the right challenges, generate alternative ideas, select ideas smartly, develop them into powerful solutions, and coaches them through to implementation. His clients include blue chip organisations across all sectors in India.

Sridhar has just published a book “Unlock The Real Power Of Ideation” – a book about how to get the best out of ideation sessions. The book has been receiving excellent reviews and response from business leaders. Sridhar has also created an iPhone/iPad app called TickleMeThink™ that helps people to think differently (www.ticklemethink.com) - available as free download in the iPhone App store.

Sridhar enjoys putting together interesting workshops and thinking tool kits. His Creative Block Busting™ Workshops are rigorous and enjoyable. (He has conducted over 400 such workshops in the last 15 years.) He anchored a 13-episode show on CNBC called “The Power of an Idea” featuring several well- known leaders from industry. His recent TEDx talk “Evict The Enemies Within” was a thought provoking one, that inspired many.

Sridhar had a distinguished career in Ogilvy & Mather for 25 long years. He started India’s first Direct Marketing agency. Subsequently he also set up Ogilvy Consulting. He was a Director of Ogilvy & Mather India, when he decided to set up his own innovation practice in 2000. His consulting firm IDEAS-RS was born in 2000. Sridhar completed a six-month distance learning programme from the Open University Business School, UK on Creativity, Innovation & Change. He is an accredited CEO Coach from the Coaching Foundation of India.

Vivek Pawar

Vivek Pawar is the Founder and Executive Chairman of Sankalp Semiconductor. He founded Sankalp in 2005 and has led the company as CEO through its start-up phase and transitioned the leadership at the scale-up mode. He enjoys industry thought leadership for his visionary approach, and people and technology leadership. He is credited for creating high tech jobs in Indian Tier-II and III cities and for structuring one of its kind Merger & Acquisition deal with KPIT Technologies Limited.

Prior to Sankalp Semiconductor, Vivek has held several executive positions at Texas Instruments India and US. He was the General Manager of Broadband Silicon Technology Centre in TI India. Several products developed under his leadership have claimed industry accolades. World's first integrated DSL that won the EDN Asia award in 1999 and World's first single chip DSL that won EDN WW Award in 2003 are few examples.

Vivek also holds other offices such as : President of North Karnataka IT Association : Vice President of TIE Hubli chapter : Trustee on Deshpande Foundation Hubli, working in social/NGO sector : Talent Development Core Interest Group (CiG) of Indian Electronics & Semiconductor Association.

Vivek received his Bachelor’s from COEP, Pune and a Master’s from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kharagpur, India.

David D’Souza

David D’Souza is a journalist and public relations consultant whose 37-year experience encompasses journalism and PR consultancy in India and Middle East. His work in journalism has led him to assignments across the world and as PR consultant directed multi-cultural teams to handle a diverse client portfolio. David moved into PR consultancy following a long stint as senior journalist with the Khaleej Times in Dubai, and Free Press Journal, Mid-Day, The Indian Post and The Daily in Mumbai, India. He joined Al-Futtaim Group as Director - Public Relations heading GMASCO's PR dept as also being PR head for the Al-Futtaim Group. He came to AFG following a stint with Bates PanGulf PR as Regional Client Leader handling a 21-member team for the Doha Asian Games. He was corporate communications manager for Emaar Properties for a very short period of time and has worked with Hill & Knowlton, Burson-Marsteller Asda’a Public Relations, Fortune Promoseven-Weber Shandwick and the Dubai World Trade Centre. His portfolio of clients includes the Australian Tourist Commission, Qatar Airways, Emaar Properties, Sorouh Developers, Dubai Festival City, IKEA, Toyota, Lexus, Honda, Volvo, Fisker, Marks & Spencer, Toys R Us, Al Futtaim Technologies, Emrill, Panasonic, Sanyo, Aftron, Dubai Department of Economic Development, Doha Asian Games Organising Committee, Qatar Tourism Authority, DHL, Porsche Middle East, Zamil Group, McDonalds, among others.

David currently has his own start-up Shabdhcraft, a PR consultancy based on Pune. He is also engaged as a visiting faculty for Symbiosis School of Sports Sciences.
