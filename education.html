India's first: Executive Program on Advanced Manufacturing & Innovation Leadership


Manufacturing plays a vital role in the creation of jobs, wealth and improving the living standards of society. India aspires to increase the contribution of the manufacturing industry to its GDP from 17 percent to 25 percent and create 100 million jobs in the next ten years. The new technologies like Sensors, Internet of Thing (IoT), 3D Printing, Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, Big Data are significantly changing the manufacturing landscape.

The program is designed to equip participants with the knowledge, tools, and understanding of all aspects of advanced manufacturing and innovation skills. The program prepares participants to become innovative future leaders in advanced manufacturing.

The combination of European and Indian expert faculty and mentors will make participants in just 12 months fully prepared to be both an effective and innovative leader in advanced manufacturing.
